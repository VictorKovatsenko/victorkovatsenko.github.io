Services

LASHES

Lashes Lamination

Lash lamination (lifting) is an innovative procedure that gives a beautiful curve to natural lashes, moisturizes, saturates them with color. The effect lasts for about 1,5-2 months.

Coloring

Lash coloring is an excellent alternative to daily mascara usage. After the procedure, the lashes appear visually brighter and longer. The result is immediately visible and lasts on average for about 4 weeks.

Protein Botox Care

Protein lashes and brows recovery contains special proteins, vitamins, and minerals that enhance hair health from the inside out. Each component of the product has its own beneficial effect: Hyaluronic acid, Keratin, Collagen, Castor oil, Tocopherol, Panthenol, Coconut extract.

Brows Lamination

Eyebrows lamination (lifting) is a long-lasting styling solution that fixes and shapes unruly eyebrow hairs. This procedure results in an aesthetically pleasing look to the eyebrows that lasts for the entire month.

Shaping

Eyebrows shaping (correction) is aimed at creating the right shape of the eyebrows that suits your individual features. There are various techniques such as tweezing or waxing depending on the skin type and your case.

Coloring

Eyebrows coloring is a process of giving the hairs the desired shade using a special dye. As a result, a natural effect is achieved.

Thinning

Brow thinning is a cosmetic procedure aimed at removing excess hairs and creating a more natural shape of the eyebrows. Thinning helps create a neater and aesthetically pleasing look for the eyebrows.
